# LISTADO: SDLC TRADICIONAL vs SDLC CON IA

---

| # | ETAPA SDLC | ROL TRADICIONAL | ROL CON IA |
|---|------------|-----------------|------------|
| 1 | **REQUISITOS** | Product Owner | **Requirements Agent** |
| 2 | **PLANIFICACIÓN** | Project Manager + Tech Lead | **Planning Agent** |
| 3 | **DISEÑO/ARQUITECTURA** | Arquitecto | **Architecture Agent** |
| 4 | **DESARROLLO/CODIFICACIÓN** | Desarrollador (Frontend + Backend) | **Code Generator Agent** |
| 5 | **INFRAESTRUCTURA** | DevOps | **Infrastructure Agent** |
| 6 | **TESTING/QA** | QA Engineer + Tester Manual | **Test Agent** |
| 7 | **SEGURIDAD** | Security Tester | **Security Agent** |
| 8 | **DESPLIEGUE** | DevOps | **Deployment Agent** |
| 9 | **MONITOREO** | SysAdmin + Operations | **Monitoring Agent** |
| 10 | **MANTENIMIENTO** | Equipo de soporte | **Maintenance Agent** |

---
